My top 3 movies of all-time.

Harry Potter Series

The world of Harry Potter encompasses many literary genres, including fantasy, drama, school story (with elements of mystery, thriller, adventure, horror, and romance). According to Rowling, the main theme of the series is life and death. Other major themes in the series are prejudice, corruption, and madness.

Lucy

Lucy is a 2014 French science fiction film directed by Luc Besson. The film questions the powers and limits of what a person can achieve if this ratio increases, based on the pseudo-theory that a normal person can use 10 percent of their brain.

Dune

In Clarke's words, "The only masterpiece that can be compared to The Lord of the Rings is fiction." The series, when considered as a whole, contains magnificent sociological, psychological and philosophical analyses and observations on human nature; institutions of religion, politics and economy; the concept of leadership; and concepts such as man, woman and sexuality.
